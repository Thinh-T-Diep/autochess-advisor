New Pieces: Grimtouch and Grand Herald

The newest additions to the game is coming very soon, promising to shake up the meta game with entire new strategies. Besides their impressive skills, they are also bringing something else with them: a brand new Class - Wizards. Read below to find out about each piece and the new class.

Grand Herald

Cost: 2 Coins
Race: God
Class: Wizard
Role: Support



Skill: Take Control
Passive: Reduces this piece's cooldown by 10%
Active: Copy the skill of the nearest centrosymmetric enemy, using this piece star level as the skill level and preserving all properties of that skill. Able to copy passive skills.
Example: If your Grand Herald is a 2-star piece and it copies the skill Artic Punch from the opposing 1-star Tusk Champion, it will have a 2-star Artic Punch. However, it will not be able to activate this skill unless an opponent get into Grand Herald's melee range.

Grimtouch

Cost: 4 Coins
Race: Demon
Class: Wizard
Role: Nuker, Support



Skill: Ink Splash
Active: Release a piercing straight ink wave, dealing 100-250-450 magical damage to the enemies, each subsequent piece take and extra 30-45-60 magical damage and marking them with an Ink Touched effect
Ink Touched: Any directional targeted skill dealt to any Ink Touched piece will also be dealt to all other Ink Touched pieces.

Wizard

Tier

Level 1: Activates when there is 2 Wizard pieces on the board

Effect

Level 1: Reduces Race/Class synergy tier by 1 piece, if that tier requires at least 4 pieces

Example

If you have 5 Goblins on your board, without Devestator, putting 2 Wizard pieces (Grand Herald and Grimtouch) on your board will activate Tier 2 Goblins race effect, which requires 6 Goblins. Additionally, it will also activate Tier 2 Mech Class effect, which requires 4 Mechs, since you would already have 3 other Mechs on board.
